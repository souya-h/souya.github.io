Souya Handa is an artist and independent curator based in Tokyo.
Handa focuses on relationship between technology and social especially how social can treat new technology with advanced moral. He consider about possibility to strengthen conservative values if high technology used with that as an artist.
On the other hand, he places himself on the edge of the history of Japan as a (ex-)high tech country.
